This tool provides an educational estimate only. Official filing must still be done through MyTax. Results are not a substitute for professional tax advice.

Who is this for?

  • Malaysian resident individuals
  • Employment income only (no business income)
  • First-time filers or anyone wanting a quick check

We estimate your tax in 5 simple steps:

1
Total Income We use the yearly income you enter.
2
Minus Reliefs We subtract eligible tax reliefs (like EPF, lifestyle, etc), up to their limits.
3
Chargeable Income This is the remaining amount after reliefs.
4
Apply Tax Rates We apply Malaysia's progressive tax rates to estimate your tax.
5
Rebate (if eligible) If your chargeable income is low, you may get a rebate that reduces your tax further.

This tool uses:

  • Manually configured Malaysian tax brackets
  • Relief limits for a specific tax year
  • Rebate rules for resident individuals
  • Your input data

It does NOT:

  • Connect to LHDN
  • Access your real financial records
  • Automatically update rules
Using YA 2025 rules
Chargeable Income = Income − Reliefs
Estimated Tax = Based on tax brackets
Final Tax = Estimated Tax − Rebate (if applicable)
